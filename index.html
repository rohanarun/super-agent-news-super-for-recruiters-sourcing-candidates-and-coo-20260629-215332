How Super fits into the recruiter AI landscape

ChatGPT

ChatGPT is excellent for drafting outreach, summarizing profiles, and answering questions. It shines for one‑off thinking tasks. For recruiters, it still requires copy‑paste between tools and repeated context setup.

Gemini

Gemini is deeply embedded in Google Workspace and, as of mid‑2026, includes native computer use in Gemini 3.5 Flash — a major signal that agents operating screens are becoming mainstream (blog.google). It helps recruiters where their work already lives, but its agent guardrails and confirmations make it less suited for long, repeated hands‑off flows.

Siri

Siri is a voice‑first assistant tightly integrated into Apple devices. It’s useful for reminders and quick actions, not for sourcing pipelines or multi‑step interview coordination.

Grok

Grok emphasizes real‑time and social context. It’s not designed around recruiter workflows or durable computer‑operating automation.

Folk

Folk and similar niche tools sit in the broader CRM and automation market. They structure data well but don’t focus on personal AI agents that can directly operate arbitrary software.

Orchids

Orchids represents more experimental approaches to automation. Useful for exploration, but not optimized for day‑to‑day recruiter operations across many legacy tools.
